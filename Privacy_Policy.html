BirdKam Snap App Privacy Policy

Thank you for using the BirdKam Snap App. This Privacy Policy explains how BirdKam Technologies ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use the BirdKam Snap App in conjunction with BirdKam cameras (collectively, the "Services"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the BirdKam Snap App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information when you use the BirdKam Snap App:

  • 1.1. Personal Information:
    • Account Information: To use the BirdKam Snap App, you may need to create an account. We collect personal information such as your name, email address, and login credentials to set up and manage your account.
    • Subscription Information: If you subscribe to cloud storage or other services through the BirdKam Snap App, we collect information related to your subscription, including payment details.
  • 1.2. Device Information:
    • Camera Data: When you use the BirdKam camera with the App, the camera may capture and upload video, audio, images, and other sensor data to our servers or cloud storage. This data is uploaded after shooting and is used to provide the Services, including AI recognition and alerts.
    • Device and Usage Information: We collect information about the devices you use to access the BirdKam Snap App, including device identifiers, IP address, device type, operating system, and usage data such as access times and the features you use.

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing and Improving Services: We use your information to operate, maintain, and improve the BirdKam Snap App and related services, including processing your requests, setting up your camera, and delivering image transmissions.
  • AI Recognition: The BirdKam Snap App may use AI technology to analyze images and video captured by the BirdKam camera. This analysis helps provide features such as identifying bird species and sending you relevant alerts.
  • Customer Support: We use your information to respond to inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Account Management: We use your information to manage your account, process subscriptions, and communicate with you about your account or subscription.
  • Security and Protection: We use your information to detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions, abuse of our services, and other illegal activities.
  • Legal Compliance: We may use your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal requests.

These service providers are required to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

4. Data Storage and Security
