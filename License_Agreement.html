  • Shooting by PIR Trigger: The camera automatically captures photos or videos when triggered by a Passive Infrared (PIR) sensor.
  • Livestreaming: Users can livestream video feeds directly from the camera.
  • Wi-Fi Connectivity: The BirdKam800 connects to the internet via a Wi-Fi network, enabling remote access and control.
